Guidelines

In the following list are a few Guidelines which should help a Creator to upload a Game with all necessary Data.
If you follow these steps below there shouldn't be any problem verifying your game!

  1. Make shure your game is in a .zip or .rar format.
  2. Upload at least one picture for your Game. Although it is not necessary it will help with the verification process.
  3. It is not necessary to upload a versions for each operation system in order to get your game verified.
  4. Try to pick your genres truthfully so the admins know what they are looking at.
  5. If your game is espacially brutal or horrifying maybe leave a hint in the description so everybody knows what they are dealing with.
  6. Try to find a catchy title so your game gets more popularity

So if your game fulfills all criteria mentioned above then there should not be any problems verifying your game.
